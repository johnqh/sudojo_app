Overview

WXYZ-Wing uses four cells with candidates forming a restricted pattern. When properly configured, a common candidate can be eliminated from cells seeing all pattern cells.

How It Works

  1. Find four cells whose combined candidates use exactly four digits {W,X,Y,Z}.
  2. One cell (pivot) sees the other three.
  3. Eliminate the restricted digit from cells seeing all four pattern cells.

Example

Highlighted cells: R8C4, R8C8, R9C7, R9C9

WXYZ-Wing pattern: R8C4={6, 9}, R8C8={8, 9}, R9C7={5, 6, 8}, R9C9={5, 6, 8}. Combined candidates: {5, 6, 8, 9}. Eliminate the restricted digit from cells seeing all four cells.

Tips & Patterns

  • WXYZ-Wings have complex requirements - study examples carefully.
  • They extend the Wing family pattern to four cells.
  • These are quite rare in most puzzles.
